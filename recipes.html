Ingredients

  • 200g poolish
  • 340g unbleached flour
  • 60g rye flour
  • 260g water
  • 50g Elrond starter
  • 9g salt
  • 25g olive oil
  • 75g dried tomatoes

Steps & Schedule

  • 18h45 (Day 1): Mix poolish and autolyse flour + water for 30 min.
  • 19h15: Add rest of ingredients and knead 10 min.
  • 19h45: Bulk ferment in fridge overnight.
  • 06h00 (Day 2): Rest 30 min at room temp.
  • 06h30: Divide and shape. Let rest 20 min.
  • 07h00: Final proof 45 min.
  • 08h00: Bake 15 min at 250°C with steam. Brush with garlic oil.

Ingredients

  • 200g poolish
  • 200g bread flour
  • 50g rye flour
  • 100g water
  • 50g Elrond starter
  • 7g salt

Steps & Schedule

Day 1 - Morning

  • Make Poolish by mixing 100g flour and 100g water, and a pinch of yeast. Leave at room temperature.
  • Prepare (and feed as required) 50g of Elrond. Leave at room temperature.

Day 1 - Evening

  • 17h15: Mix flour and water (autolyse).
  • 17h45: Add starter + salt. Knead.
  • Stretch & fold x4 over 2h.
  • Cold proof overnight.

Day 2 - Morning

  • 07h40: Shape and detent for 20 min. NOTE: if it spent the night in canneton, it should not be reshaped.
  • 08h00: Score.
  • 08h00: Bake at 475°F with steam for 35–40 min.

Old Fashioned Slab

A hearty, grain-rich artisan bread inspired by Le Cordon Bleu’s traditional formula. This dense and flavorful loaf is built with a mix of flours, toasted seeds, and a robust stiff levain named Moria. Perfect for breakfast or pairing with cheese and charcuterie.

Ingredients

  • Grain Mix:
  • 100g Sunflower seeds
  • 100g Brown flaxseeds
  • 100g Water
  • Dough:
  • 200g Bread flour
  • 100g Spelt flour
  • 100g Whole-wheat flour
  • 50g Rye flour
  • 50g All-purpose flour
  • 15g Salt
  • 3g Fresh yeast
  • 300g Grain mix (prepared earlier)
  • 380g Water (room temp)
  • 300g Stiff levain (Moria)
  • To finish: Oats (QS)

Steps & Schedule

Morning

  • Toast grains at 350°F (175°C) for 4–5 min in convection oven.
  • Immediately plunge toasted grains in water, stir, and refrigerate to cool.

Midday

  • Place water, stiff levain, flours, and yeast in mixer with hook.
  • Mix on speed 1. After 30 sec, add salt. Mix 2–3 min more on speed 1.
  • Switch to speed 3 for 5 min. Add grain mix, mix 1 min on speed 1, then 3 until distributed.
  • Shape dough into smooth ball, cover and proof at 30°C for 45–60 min.

Afternoon

  • Degas dough, divide into 3x 420g pieces. Pre-shape into balls. Rest 10 min.
  • Shape slightly like eggs, place seam-up in linen-lined baskets. Final proof 45–60 min at 30°C.

Baking

  • Preheat oven at max temperature.
  • Score.
  • Steam oven (half a cup on bottom).
  • Bake: 475F for 18 min.
  • Rotate, bake 5–6 min more.
