Scenario Planning (SP)

Description:

Scenario planning involves creating detailed narratives about different future environments in which your organization might operate. This method helps in understanding how different organizational structures would perform under various hypothetical scenarios.

Application:

By evaluating how each proposed structure might fare under potential future conditions (e.g., market changes, technological advancements), you can identify the most resilient and adaptable options.

Future Environments:

  • Future Environment A: STC on an older aircraft, consisting of an engine instrument glass panel on a DHC-7. Requires system level qualification (DO-254, DO-160) and aircraft level certification, including systems and equipment and flight test.
  • Future Environment B: STC on an aircraft that will require equipment certification, including structure, flight test and flight manual.
  • Future Environment C: Need to change the engineering process manual and the configuration management process document. Will need to get approval of these changes by Transport Canada regional engineer.
